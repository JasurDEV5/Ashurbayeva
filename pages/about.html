About the Book

The study guide presents a modern view of the problem of psychology and pedagogy in medicine and higher medical education. This book will help students navigate the main categories of psychology and pedagogy, understand the goals, principles, methods and forms of education in a medical university, learn to identify pedagogical and psychological aspects in the work of a doctor, deeply understand the role of general psychology and pedagogy as humanitarian knowledge in professional and personal development improve your professional culture.
